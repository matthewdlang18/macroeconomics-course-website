Weekly Learning Structure

Lectures

Mondays & Wednesdays, 12:30-1:45 PM in Campbell Hall

  • Take notes during lecture
  • Participate in real-time TopHat questions
  • Ask questions during the lecture
  • Review difficult topics after lecture

Discussion Sections

AI-based activities to connect with peers

  • Complete and submit the assigned activity by the end of section
  • Your top 3 (of 5) activities count for 15% of your grade
  • Attendance and participation are mandatory for credit

Lecture Quizzes

Released Wednesdays, due before next Monday's lecture

  • Based on previous week's material
  • One chance to answer questions
  • 40-50 correct answers needed for full credit (10% of grade)

Review Sessions

Thursdays, 5-5:50 PM and 6-6:50 PM in North Hall 1110

  • Focus on lengthy, difficult word problems
  • Not mandatory but helpful for deeper understanding
  • Led by teaching assistants

Office Hours

Professor Lang: Wednesdays, 2:00-3:30 PM in North Hall 3032

Coffee Hours: Fridays, 1:00-2:00 PM (Rotating Location)

Sign up for Coffee Hours at: https://shorturl.at/eYOXy
