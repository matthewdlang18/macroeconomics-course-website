Investment Tips

  • Diversify your portfolio - Don't put all your eggs in one basket.
  • Consider asset correlations - Assets that move together offer less diversification benefit.
  • Watch for market shocks - Sudden price changes can create buying opportunities.
  • Rebalance regularly - Adjust your portfolio as market conditions change.
  • Use the "Distribute Cash Evenly" feature - A quick way to diversify your investments.
