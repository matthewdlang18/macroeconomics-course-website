How to Play

Guess the economics term in 6 tries or less:

  • Each guess must match the number of letters in the term
  • Green tiles show correct letters in the right position
  • Yellow tiles show correct letters in the wrong position
  • Gray tiles show letters that aren't in the term
  • Multi-word terms and spaces are supported

Scoring

  • Fewer attempts = more points
  • Longer terms = more points
  • Multi-word terms = bonus points
  • Longer streaks = more points
