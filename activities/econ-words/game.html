How to Play

Guess the economics term in 6 tries or less:

  • Correct letters in right position
  • Correct letters in wrong position
  • Letters not in the term
  • Each guess must match the term length
  • Multi-word terms and spaces are supported

Scoring

  • +100 points per remaining attempt
  • +50 points per letter in term
  • +100 points per word (multi-word bonus)
  • +25 points per streak level
  • Time bonus up to 1500 points
